VIT Projects

SDP (Skill Development Program)

SDP focuses on equipping students with industry-ready skills through hands-on training and workshops. These programs cover advanced technologies like AI, ML, and Cloud Computing.

EDI (Entrepreneurship Development Initiative)

EDI fosters innovation and entrepreneurship among students by encouraging them to develop business ideas and startups. It includes mentoring sessions and business model creation.

DT (Design Thinking)

DT enables students to tackle complex problems creatively by using structured design thinking methodologies. It emphasizes empathy, ideation, and prototyping.

Course Projects

Course projects are part of the curriculum, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios. These projects enhance problem-solving and teamwork skills.
